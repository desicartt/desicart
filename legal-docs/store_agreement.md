1. **GoJack Pty Ltd** (ACN: ****\_\_****) of **************\_\_************** (“GoJack”), and
2. **Store Partner:** **************\_\_************** (Legal entity name) (ABN/ACN: ****\_\_****) of **************\_\_************** (“Store”).

## 1. Appointment

GoJack appoints Store as a non‑exclusive supplier of products for sale and delivery to GoJack customers via the GoJack platform.

## 2. Pricing model

- **Supply Price:** set by Store (what GoJack pays Store).
- **Retail Price:** set by GoJack (what customers pay).
- Store acknowledges Retail Price is controlled by GoJack and may differ from Supply Price.

## 3. Orders and fulfilment

Store must:

- Pick and pack items accurately and hygienically.
- Use appropriate packaging and maintain cold chain where required.
- Mark orders “Ready” in the dashboard and follow QR handoff procedures as required by GoJack.

## 4. Quality and compliance

Store warrants all goods are fit for sale and comply with applicable laws including food safety obligations.
Store must provide allergen information where relevant and follow any GoJack operational standards reasonably notified.

## 5. Payments and settlement

- Settlement frequency: **weekly** (e.g., Tuesdays) unless otherwise agreed.
- Settlement amount: delivered items × Supply Price, less agreed adjustments (refunds/chargebacks/quality issues).
- GoJack may maintain a reasonable holdback (e.g., 2%) to cover refunds and chargebacks.

## 6. Refunds, defects, and disputes

GoJack controls customer refunds to maintain customer experience.
Where issues arise due to Store fault (wrong items, spoilage, expiry), GoJack may:

- deduct the refund amount from settlement; and/or
- apply reasonable defect penalties as set out in GoJack policies provided to Store.

## 7. Term and termination

- Initial term: **12 months**, auto‑renewing unless terminated with notice.
- Either party may terminate for material breach if not cured within a reasonable period (or immediately for severe breach, fraud, or safety issues).

## 8. Confidentiality

Store must sign and comply with GoJack’s NDA and treat GoJack information, customer data, and platform procedures as confidential.

## 9. Data ownership

- Customer data and platform analytics are owned by GoJack.
- Store may only access customer information required to fulfil orders.

## 10. Liability

Each party is responsible for its own negligence and legal compliance.
Limitations and exclusions apply to the maximum extent permitted by law.

## 11. Electronic signing

The parties consent to signing electronically.
