This Independent Contractor Agreement (**Agreement**) is made between:

1. **GoJack Pty Ltd** (ACN: ****\_\_****) of **************\_\_************** (“GoJack”), and
2. **Driver (Contractor):** **************\_\_************** of **************\_\_************** (“Driver”).

## 1. Independent contractor

Driver is an independent contractor and not an employee. Driver is responsible for their own taxes, insurance, and compliance obligations.

## 2. Engagement

GoJack may offer delivery jobs via the app. Driver may accept or decline jobs. GoJack does not guarantee minimum work.

## 3. Fees and payment

Driver fees are set by GoJack’s fee schedule (distance/time/batching). Payments are settled on the schedule communicated in the app/dashboard.

## 4. QR chain-of-custody (mandatory)

Driver must follow GoJack pickup and delivery verification steps, including QR scanning where required, to:

- confirm correct pickup and delivery; and
- reduce fraud and disputes.

Repeated failure to comply may result in non‑payment for affected deliveries and/or deactivation.

## 5. Conduct and customer privacy

Driver must:

- behave respectfully;
- comply with road laws;
- protect customer privacy and only use customer data for completing the delivery; and
- not contact customers outside the GoJack platform unless required for delivery and permitted by GoJack policy.

## 6. Vehicle and insurance

Driver is responsible for ensuring their vehicle (if used) is roadworthy and appropriately insured for their delivery activities.

## 7. Performance and deactivation

GoJack may use performance standards (on‑time rate, completion, complaints, fraud indicators) and may suspend/deactivate accounts for safety, fraud, misconduct, or repeated performance failures.

## 8. Term and termination

This agreement continues until terminated. Either party may terminate with notice (as per GoJack policy) and GoJack may suspend immediately for serious breach.

## 9. Confidentiality

Driver must sign and comply with GoJack’s NDA.

## 10. Electronic signing

The parties consent to signing electronically.
