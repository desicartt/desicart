## 1. Definitions

**Confidential Information** includes all non‑public information disclosed by GoJack, including but not limited to:

- Product, operational and process information.
- Customer data and order data.
- Pricing, margins, revenue model, costs, and financials.
- Batching, routing, anti‑fraud and QR chain-of-custody workflows.
- Any information marked confidential or that a reasonable person would treat as confidential.

**Trade Secrets** include any Confidential Information deriving value from not being generally known (including algorithmic decisioning, heuristics, and operational methods).

## 2. Confidentiality obligations

The Recipient must:

- Keep Confidential Information strictly confidential.
- Use Confidential Information only for the permitted purpose: evaluating, onboarding, and/or performing services with GoJack.
- Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without GoJack’s written consent.
- Ensure any permitted personnel with access are bound by equivalent confidentiality obligations.

## 3. Prohibited conduct (examples)

- Copying GoJack’s operational model to build a competing service.
- Using customer information outside assigned deliveries/orders.
- Disclosing GoJack pricing strategy, margins, or internal dashboards.

## 4. Exclusions

Confidential Information does not include information that the Recipient can prove:

- Is public through no breach by the Recipient; or
- Was already lawfully known by the Recipient; or
- Is independently developed without reference to GoJack’s information; or
- Is lawfully received from a third party without restriction.

## 5. Term

- Confidential Information: **5 years** from disclosure.
- Trade Secrets: **indefinite** while they remain trade secrets under law.
- Customer data: confidential while Recipient is engaged with GoJack and thereafter as required by law.

## 6. Return / deletion

Upon request or termination, Recipient must promptly return or permanently delete all Confidential Information (including copies) and confirm in writing.

## 7. Remedies

Recipient acknowledges breach may cause irreparable harm. GoJack may seek injunctive relief and damages, and recover reasonable enforcement costs as permitted by law.

## 8. Governing law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of Victoria, Australia.

## 9. Electronic signing

The parties consent to signing electronically and agree that electronic signatures satisfy any signature requirement to the maximum extent permitted by law.
